Welcome to the relatively interactive URL based take, on Rock, Paper, Scissors

So just how do we play this game?

In normal Rock, Paper, Scissors, there are two players who use hand actions to choose to play either a Rock, Paper, or Scissors. They draw at the same time as one another, and the following rules apply:

  • Rock beats Scissors
  • Paper beats Rock
  • Scissors beats paper

The fun twist for this game however, is that not only is it played in your browser, it's played via the URL.

The current URL you are on is http://localhost:5000/rps/

In order for Player 1 to choose their Rock, Paper, or Scissors, they must enter it into the url after 'rps/', for example: http://localhost:5000/rps/scissors/

And for Player 2, they must enter their choice after Player 1. http://localhost:5000/rps/scissors/rock

The result of this game, is Rock beats Scissors, meaning Player 2 wins the game! And so on.
